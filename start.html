Rock Paper Scissors Lizard Spock

Welcome to a game of an old classic - with a twist!

How to play this game:

Choose one of the five options, Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, or Spock by clicking on the appropriate button.

Simultaniously, the computer's choice will be randomly generated.

If you win, 1 point will be added to your score.

If both players choose the same variable, it's a tie. Even though everybody wins, you will not gain a point.

If you lose, don't panic, you will not lose a point.

After you have won 10 rounds, you will become the ultimate champion.

Rules:

Each option can beat or be beaten by two other options as follows:

  • Scissors cuts Paper
  • Paper covers Rock
  • Rock crushes Lizard
  • Lizard poisons Spock
  • Spock smashes Scissors
  • Scissors decapitates Lizard
  • Lizard eats Paper
  • Paper disproves Spock
  • Spock vaporizes Rock
  • (and as it always has) Rock crushes Scissors
